Answers to frequently asked questions
What bonuses are there for birthday people?

We personally congratulate each birthday person with a piece of an amazing cake from our pastry chef, and if you plan to celebrate your birthday in a company where there will be more than 12 people, then we will give you a whole cake!

* only when making a pre-order, and specifying the number of people during the preparation of the order (so that we have time to prepare a cake for you).

Does your restaurant have parking?

You can use the free underground parking for our guests. To get to the parking lot, drive up to the gate, call the hostess at +1 (267) 846-26-71, and you will be immediately opened.

Is it possible to hold a banquet in your restaurant?

Yes, we offer our guests such an option for almost any event format. To clarify all the details, call +1 (267) 846-26-71, and our managers will listen to your wishes, ask clarifying questions and offer a choice of several options for your celebration. We will help you avoid traditional mistakes when organizing a banquet and will do everything to ensure that your event is held at the highest level.

Do you have separate rooms for small companies?

Yes, we can offer you a room/rooms for private events or meetings for 10 people. You can book such a special room by phone +1 (267) 846-26-71.

Is it possible to come to you with a dog?

As you know, a dog is a friend of a person, and any person is our guest. And we love the friends of our guests and will be glad to see you in the company of your four-legged pets.

Do you have live music?

We invite musicians to perform at some special events. In order not to miss such parties, we advise you to subscribe to the newsletter, and then you will be among the first invited.
